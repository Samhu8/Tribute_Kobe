The Black Mamba

"The most important thing is to try and inspire people so that they can be great in whatever they want to do" - Kobe Bryant

Kobe Bryant was a professional basketball player who played for the Los Angeles Lakers for his entire 20 year career. Known as one of the greatest basketball players to every play the game, Kobe inspired an entire generation of fans across the world with his "Mamba Mentality." He tragically passed away on January 26, 2020 with his daughter Gianna and 7 other passengers in a helicopter accident. Though his life was cut short, he left behind an amazing family, career, and lessons for all to gain wisdom from, work hard and you can achieve anything.
