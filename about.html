Where it all began

Before being known as the "Black "Mamba", Kobe Bean Bryant was born on August 23,1978, in Philadelphia, PA. He was the son of former NBA player Joe Bryant. Due to his father playing overseas, Kobe grew up a few years in Italy. He went to school in Lower Merion High School where he began to solidfy himself as a top American high-school player.

Personal Life

Kobe was the youngest of three children. He was the only son of Joe Bryant and his mother, Pamela Cox Bryant. He was married to his wife, Vanessa Bryant from 2001 - 2020. Together they had 4 daughters, Natalia, Gianna, Bianka and Capri Bryant. After his basketball career ended, he began his second career as being a storyteller. He made books for kids called The Wizenard Series where he teaches team sports and individual growth. He even won an Oscar for his short story, Dear Basketball.
